# liquidsoap-radioplus-and-EMF

I have built this because I wanted to ensure a full professional stream with regards relaying Radio Plus.

Radio Plus has been using Icecast but has failed to actually insert their song and artist titles, so this project allowed me to change transcode their existing stream onto my own Icecast server and make a custom song title. 

I also wanted to bring K-Love and Air 1 back into Icecast feeds by converting their HLS feeds so I could use my Barix Exstreamer to play back K-Love and Air 1 feeds.

I live in a group home / assisted living so my space is very limited, I had to come up with a unique way of listening to my favorite stations by placing them into an equipment rack that was approved my by medical team.

I use 8 Barix Exstreamers 4 of which are in use by Radio Plus feeds and the other 4 are for K-Love feeds - so I can listen to these stations - I have a rack mounted audio switcher, with 8 channels so I can literally flip from 0 through 7 to listen to each one at anytime. 

This tutorial shows you how I did exactly that.
